Motion Analysis of Baseball Pitching

Developed an advanced computer vision system to analyze baseball pitching performance and risk. Utilized the rtmlib model for high-precision pose estimation, followed by sophisticated data filtering and smoothing. The system extracts bio-mechanical features to evaluate technique and predict potential injury risks.

Problem: Lack of accessible, automated tools for bio-mechanical pitch analysis.
Approach: rtmlib Pose Estimation, Savitzky-Golay filtering, Feature Engineering.
Results: Accurate biomechanical insights and risk assessment from standard video input.
Python rtmlib OpenCV SciPy NumPy
